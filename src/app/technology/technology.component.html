Google will no longer read your email to serve you ads

Privacy advocates have long criticized Google's practice of using email data to tailor advertising, but now the company is putting an end to the method. Google (GOOG) announced the change in a blog post on Friday. It will go into effect later this year The move is in line with Google's existing G Suite Gmail service for businesses, which doesn't scan email data for personalized ads.
